# IBM-Cognos-Hospitality-Revenue-Analytics
Hotel Revenue Analysis using IBM Cognos Analytics to visualize revenue, occupancy, and guest insights. Includes reports, dashboards, exploration, and storytelling to support data-driven decisions in hospitality management.

## Modules Covered

### Q1 – Reports
- Revenue Report by Property  
- Room, F&B, Spa & Total Revenue Analysis  
- Conditional Styling & Prompts  

### Q2 – Occupancy Analysis
- Room Occupancy Report  
- Occupancy % by Room Type & Month  
- Performance Highlights (Best/Worst Months)  

### Q3 – Revenue Trends
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis  
- Bar/Line Chart Selection  
- YTD Revenue & MoM Growth  

### Q4 – Deep Exploration
- Length of Stay vs Total Bill Analysis  
- Booking Channel Performance  
- Guest Rating vs Room Insights  

### Q5 – Executive Dashboard
- KPI Tiles (Revenue, Occupancy, Rating, RevPAR)  
- Bar, Line, Pie Visualizations  
- Top Guests & Filters  

### Q6 – Data Storytelling
- Revenue & Occupancy Overview  
- Property Performance Comparison  
- F&B & Ancillary Revenue  
- Guest Satisfaction Trends  
- Seasonal Patterns & Strategy
